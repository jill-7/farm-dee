Our Services

What we Offer

Quality Services for Livestock and Ornamental Birds"

  • Farm Tours

    Immerse yourself in the wonders of sustainable farming through our enlightening farm tours, where you'll witness firsthand the magic of farm in action.

  • Workshops and Training

    Dee's Farm conducts workshops and training programs, educating participants on various aspects of livestock and ornamental bird farming,

  • Farming Consultation

    Dee's Farm offers consultation services to farmers, providing guidance on livestock and ornamental bird farming, including breeding, nutrition, and general management practices.

  • Livestock Sales

    Dee's Farm offers a variety of livestock for sale, including pigs, dairy goats, goats, and day-old chicks

  • Ornamental Bird Sales

    Customers can purchase a range of ornamental birds, such as ducks, bantams, taifa birds, guinea fowls, and turkeys, to enhance their farms or gardens.

  • Breeding Services

    Dee's Farm provides specialized breeding services, assisting customers with the reproduction and breeding of their livestock or ornamental birds, including services specifically tailored for goats and pigs on heat.
