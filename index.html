We Believe In Working Accredited Farmers

At Dee's Farm, we believe in the power of farming to create a remarkable impact on both individuals and communities. Step into our world, where we cultivate a vision of success through small-scale farming and inspire the next generation of agricultural entrepreneurs.

Livestock Farming

Dee's Farm specializes in raising healthy pigs and offering a variety of livestock such as young dairy goats, goats, and piglets for sale.

Ornamental Birds

Dee's Farm breeds and sells a stunning selection of Poultry farming (Kienyeji Chickens, Ducks, Guinea fowls, Turkeys...), Ornamental birds (Bantams, Taifa birds...) and Layers (Kienyeji chicken, Bantams) adding beauty and charm to farms and gardens.
